Sales Performance Dashboard

Problem

The logistics team lacked visibility into carrier performance and delivery efficiency. While shipment data was available, there was no clear way to compare carriers fairly, understand cost behavior over time, or identify inefficient routes and delivery risks. Most metrics focused only on delivery time or total cost, which failed to explain why certain carriers or routes performed better than others.

Data & Tools

  • Data sources:Company sales database
  • Data volume: ~2M rows
  • Tools: Power BI (Modeling & Visualization) DAX (Advanced KPI calculations)

What I Built

  • Data Modeling & KPI Design * Designed calculated measures (not columns) to ensure metrics adapt dynamically to filters * Focused on time-normalized and distance-normalized KPIs for fair comparison
  • * Distance Covered per Day Measures carrier productivity * Total Cost per Day Measures spending velocity * Transit Efficiency Index Measures route efficiency
  • Carrier-Level Performance Analysis * Compared carriers using: Distance per day Cost per day Transit efficiency * Corrected misleading totals by applying context-aware DAX logic * Ensured totals represent business-meaningful averages, not recalculated raw values

Key Insights

  • Some carriers covered more distance per day while maintaining lower cost per day, indicating higher operational efficiency
  • Certain carriers appeared fast but were cost-inefficient when analyzed per day
  • Total cost and transit days alone were misleading without normalization

Key Highlights

  • Advanced DAX-based KPI design
  • Carrier productivity & cost efficiency analysis
  • Business-focused storytelling with real insights
